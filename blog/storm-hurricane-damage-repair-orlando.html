Storm & Hurricane Damage Repair in Orlando

Florida storms can leave behind roof damage, water intrusion, and structural issues. Acting quickly and safely after a storm helps limit further damage to your Orlando home.

First steps after a storm

  • Stay safe and avoid damaged or flooded areas
  • Document damage with photos
  • Cover openings to prevent more water intrusion
  • Get a professional inspection

Common storm-related repairs

Roof leaks, water damage, damaged siding, and moisture inside walls are all common after severe weather. Addressing them promptly prevents mold and structural problems.
